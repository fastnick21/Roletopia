QUESTIONS

Before you download

Is Roletopia playable right now?+

Not yet. The architecture and website exist, but the Among Us IL2CPP integration and playable role implementations are still being built.

Will console and mobile players be supported?+

The goal is for vanilla clients to join a compatible host, but this must be proven through real game integration and platform testing before it can be promised.

What do the download numbers mean?+

They total actual downloads of files attached to public GitHub releases. They are not an estimated player count.
